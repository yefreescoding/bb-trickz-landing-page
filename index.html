Who's She?

Bb trickz is a Spanish artist born in Barcelona in 2000 and real name Belize Kazi. His style is characterized by controversial and sometimes even humorous lyrics, and a flow offbeat on rhythms generally of drill, Detroit or even R & B. This has led him to receive numerous comparisons mainly with the American artist Ice Spice.

BB Trickz: "Urban Music shops in Humana"

The 23-year-old Catalan has revolutionized everything she has found in her path. From zero to one hundred. One day it appeared on the Twitter timeline and, minutes later, no one stopped talking about it. Passivity, fashion, exaltation of conventional beauty, lyrics impossible to explain to a person who is in his thirties and a celebration of everything that makes generation Z unique. A cocktail that merges with the classic urban codes, which it does not abandon, but it does transform into its own language
